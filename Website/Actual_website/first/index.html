Home

I am currently studying for my PhD in Algebraic Geometry at Southampton University under Dr. Bernhard Koeck. In particular we are looking at group actions on algebraic curves. We are currently finalising a paper computing when the action on the space of holomorphic differentials of curves is faithful.

I completed my MMath at Warwick University in 2010. My masters project was on algebraic K-theory, and can be found at the work page.
